Chicken Soup

Description

This caldo de pollo recipe is not only hearty and nutritious — it’s a bowlful of delicious comfort.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Water
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Step 1:Boil the water, chicken, and seasonings (besides the chicken bouillon) together.
  2. Step 2:Reduce to a simmer and cook until the meat falls off the bone.
  3. Step 3:Stir in the bouillon and vegetables, then simmer until the veggies are tender.
  4. Step 4:Stir in the cilantro.
